Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2025

Welcome to Amar Golpo Ghor! This Privacy Policy describes how Amar Golpo Ghor ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our website (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We believe in minimalism and respecting your privacy. Currently, Amar Golpo Ghor collects very limited information:

  • Local Storage Data: We use your browser's local storage to save your preferences (e.g., theme, font size, line height) and your "My Books" list (favorited books). This data is stored directly on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Anonymous Usage Data (Hypothetical): In the future, we *may* implement anonymous analytics to understand how the site is used (e.g., most popular books, pages visited). This would be non-personally identifiable information. If we do, this policy will be updated, and you will be informed.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we collect minimal personal information, our use is also minimal:

  • To personalize your reading experience (theme, font size, line height).
  • To maintain your "My Books" list locally on your device.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your locally stored data with third parties. As no personal data is collected on our servers, there is no server-side data to disclose.

4. Data Security

While we do not collect sensitive personal information, we strive to protect any information locally stored on your device from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

  • Local Storage: You can clear your browser's local storage at any time through your browser settings. This will remove your saved preferences and "My Books" list from your device.
  • Cookies: Currently, Amar Golpo Ghor does not use traditional cookies for tracking.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
